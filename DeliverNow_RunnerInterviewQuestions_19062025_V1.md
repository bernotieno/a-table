# DeliverNow UX Research Runner Survey  

## Runner Survey Questions  

### 1️ Background Info  
- How long have you worked as a runner?  
- How many deliveries do you usually complete in a shift?  

### 2️ Route Allocation  
- How do you currently get your delivery assignments?  
- Do you feel the way routes are assigned is fair? Why or why not?  
- What problems do you face with your assigned routes (e.g. traffic, distance, order batching)?  

### 3️ Task Clarity  
- When you receive a delivery task, how clear are the instructions (e.g. pick-up location, delivery address, special requests)?  
- Have you ever had trouble understanding what to do on a delivery? Please describe.  

### 4️ Tools + Communication  
- How easy is it to contact your manager if you have a problem during delivery?  
- What features in an app would make your job easier?  

### 5️ General Experience  
- What frustrates you the most during deliveries?  
- What do you like about your current delivery experience?
