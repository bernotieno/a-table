# DeliverNow UX Research Runner Manager Survey  quesions

### 1️ Background Info  
- How many runners do you typically oversee during a shift?  
- What tools do you currently use to manage runners?  

### 2️ Monitoring + Control  
- How do you track the location and status of runners?  
- What challenges do you face in tracking or managing your runners?  
- How do you assign or reassign delivery tasks?  

### 3️ Route Fairness + Efficiency  
- How do you ensure routes are fairly distributed?  
- What difficulties arise in managing route assignments?  

### 4️ Communication  
- How do you communicate with runners during deliveries?  
- What tools or features would help you manage your team better?  

### 5️ General Experience  
- What do you like about your current runner management system?  
- What is your biggest frustration as a runner manager?
